Our Story Began On A Mountain

NorthPeak started with one simple mission — build outdoor gear that adventurers can trust anywhere on earth. From early morning alpine hikes to multi-day treks through remote wilderness, we design equipment that performs when it matters most. What began as a small group of hikers testing gear in harsh conditions has grown into a global community of explorers.
