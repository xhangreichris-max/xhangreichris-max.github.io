Common Questions

Does it taste like mushrooms?

expand_more
Not at all. Our extract process removes the earthy fungal taste while preserving all the beneficial compounds. It tastes like a rich, dark roasted coffee with subtle nutty undertones.

Is there any caffeine in it?

expand_more
Our standard blend contains approximately 45mg of caffeine—about half of a regular cup—sourced from organic green coffee beans, which works synergistically with L-Theanine.

Can I drink it if I'm pregnant?

expand_more
While our ingredients are natural, we always recommend consulting with your healthcare provider before introducing new supplements into your diet during pregnancy.
