Campaign Case Study • Mushroom Coffee Ritual

What I built for this campaign

This portfolio project demonstrates an end-to-end email campaign workflow: a landing page with email capture (inline + exit-intent popup), a 5-email welcome sequence plan, and QA documentation using client preview screenshots.

Primary objective

Capture new subscribers and warm them up across 5 days with value-first content and a clear CTA.

My role

Email developer: layout breakdown, table-based HTML builds, Outlook-safe fallbacks, testing + QA notes.

Inline signup + exit intent popup 5-email welcome sequence structure Outlook-safe approach (MSO/VML patterns) testi.at QA screenshots (current)
